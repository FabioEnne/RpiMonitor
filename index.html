Welcome to Rpi Control Panel Page

Easy control is the real power

RCP (Rpi Control Panel) is a new born idea with the aim to simplify the use of you Rpi while you are not home, control your Rpi simply from a browser, from your mobile or from a PC.

Your Rpi at a glance

The goal we are trying to achive via the development of this WebApp is to let you easily see your Rpi Status and usage, kill the process you don't need, manage your files everything from your fingertips, those are the first features we want to implement! Many othere features can be thinked and implemented in the future, this is the power of the open surce! Feel free to create a new branch and help us out!!
